## Passos sugeridos

1. In `core/src/lib.rs` add cfg-free (NO `#[cfg]`, must compile in wasm) UniFFI functions delegating to `the_light_core::userdata::plans`: (a) `list_reading_plans() -> Vec<ReadingPlanSummary>` wrapping `plans::available_plans()` + `plans::plan_by_id(id).map(|p| p.len())`; new pure Record `ReadingPlanSummary{ id:String, name:String, days:u32 }`.
