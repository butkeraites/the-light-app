## Objetivo

reading-sample.sqlite is ~13.74MB but ~9MB of it is lexicon (original_tokens/lexicon) needed ONLY by opt-in deep-study/lexical-entries. Pure readers who never touch AI still download it. Split the web reading data: generate a reading-lite subset (verses+xref+FTS, ~4.4MB) that the reading/search/xref path loads, and load the lexicon subset (~9MB) on demand ONLY when deep-study or lexical-entries runs (opt-in). Add a visible on-demand loading indicator for the (now clearly deferred) reading DB fetch.

> **Herda F5.3 (defer/métrica) + F5.9/F5.12 (code-split do GLUE do léxico).** F5.9 splitou o
> JS do léxico; F5.15 separa o **DADO** (~9MB) do caminho de LEITURA: carrega o léxico só
> **on-demand** (ao abrir estudo/léxico), com UX de carregamento. A leitura deve funcionar sem
> o léxico. REUSA a métrica da F5.3 e ATUALIZA a baseline (o dado do léxico sai do caminho
> crítico). _Blocking:_ **sim** (define o 'verde' do workstream perf). NÃO reimplementar o léxico (dado do store STEP CC-BY).

## Restrições

Biggest DATA-path win for pure readers (~9MB), and it aligns with offline-first/opt-in (lexicon is an AI concern). Highest-effort/riskiest task: touches the app-side generator + two web stores + asset wiring; keep it WEB-SCOPED (native stays on the combined DB) to remain atomic. sqlite-lexicon.web.ts currently REUSES the reading handle — that coupling must be broken so lexicon has its own on-demand file. Anti-hallucination: verse text still verbatim from the lite store; glosses/attribution from the lexicon store; no drift.

- **Offline-first:** o léxico separado continua asset LOCAL, carregado on-demand (opt-in ao abrir estudo/léxico); a LEITURA funciona 100% offline SEM o léxico. Nada exige rede.
- **Anti-alucinação/zero-drift:** separar o DADO do léxico do caminho de leitura NÃO muda o conteúdo — quando carregado, glosas/Strong são idênticos (STEP CC-BY do store). Self-tests idênticos.
- **the-light só via PR+ADR:** `the-light-core` pinado (`2fc2dab`); app-side (asset/bundle/loading).
- **Qualidade:** `tsc --noEmit`; expo export web 0; TODOS os `test:web:*` (esp. lexicon/study) sem regressão; wasm puro; **re-center + commit da baseline** (o léxico sai do caminho de leitura → medir e atualizar `measure-web-bundle.sh`+baseline → BUDGET OK).
